# Set up your organisation

Your organisation is the home for everything else. Teams live inside it, and competitions are run from it. You'll set this up once, then build on top of it.

## Before you start

Decide what type of organisation you're creating. MatchPulse has four:

- **School**
- **Club**
- **Association**
- **Franchise**

This choice shapes how your organisation behaves and how it connects to others. If you're not sure which fits, read [Org types explained](../02-organisations-teams/org-types.md) first.

## Create it

1. From your dashboard, click **Create organisation**.
2. Choose the organisation type.
3. Enter the organisation name.
4. Fill in any other details requested (logo, location, and so on).
5. Save.

Your organisation now appears on your dashboard, and you're its Owner.

## What's next

With your organisation in place, you can:

- [Add teams to it](../02-organisations-teams/add-a-team.md).
- [Link it to a parent organisation](../02-organisations-teams/link-a-parent-organisation.md), if it belongs to a wider body.
- [Create a competition](../03-competitions/create-a-competition.md).

If your organisation sits under a bigger one, such as a school that's part of an association, set up the parent link before you start running shared competitions. It keeps everything connected properly.
