# The dashboard explained

The dashboard is your home base. It's the first thing you see after signing in, and it's where you get to everything else.

## What's on it

- **Your organisations.** The schools, clubs or associations you belong to or run. If you're new, you'll create your first one here.
- **Your competitions.** Any competition you own or have a role in, with quick access to its fixtures and standings.
- **Upcoming and recent fixtures.** A view of what's coming up and what's just been played, so you can jump straight into scoring.

## Moving around

From the dashboard you can drill down in a logical order: pick an organisation, see its teams and competitions, open a competition, and from there reach its fixtures and standings. You can always get back to the dashboard from the main navigation.

## What you'll see depends on your role

The dashboard only shows you what you have access to. If someone gave you a Scorer role on one competition, you'll see that competition and its fixtures, but not the rest of their setup. Owners see everything they own. More on this in [Roles explained](../08-permissions/roles-explained.md).
