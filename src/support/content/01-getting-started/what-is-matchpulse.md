# What is MatchPulse

MatchPulse is a platform for running hockey competitions, from a single school fixture to a full league season. You set up your teams, build a competition, schedule the fixtures, and score matches live. Standings, player stats and career records build themselves from the results as they come in.

## What you can do with it

- Run leagues, knockout tournaments, or a mix of both.
- Add and schedule fixtures, including bulk fixture generation.
- Score matches live, with goals and events recorded as they happen.
- Track standings that update automatically from finalised results.
- Keep player and career stats across seasons.
- Give other people access to score or manage, with control over who can change what.

## Who it's for

Schools, clubs, associations and franchises all use MatchPulse a little differently. A school might run its internal fixtures and feed into a wider association league. A club might manage several teams across age groups. The platform handles all of these through one shared structure, which you'll set up when you create your organisation.

## How the pieces fit together

There's a natural order to things:

1. You create an **organisation** (your school, club, or association).
2. Inside it, you create **teams**.
3. You build a **competition** and add teams to it.
4. You add **fixtures**, the individual matches.
5. You score those fixtures, and the results feed your **standings and stats**.

Each of these has its own section in this support centre. If you're starting fresh, the next step is to [create your account](create-your-account.md).
