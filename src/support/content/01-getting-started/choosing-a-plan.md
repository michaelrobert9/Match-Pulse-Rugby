# Choosing a plan: Free, Plus, Pro

MatchPulse has three plans. You can start on Free and upgrade whenever you need more.

## Free

Everything you need to set up an organisation, create teams, build a competition and score matches. A good place to start and find your feet.

## Plus — R2,000 once-off

A one-time payment, not a subscription. Pay once and the Plus features stay unlocked. Suited to a club or school that runs competitions regularly and wants more than the Free plan offers without an annual commitment.

## Pro — R15,000 per year

The full platform, billed annually. Built for associations and larger operations running multiple competitions across a season. If you're managing a lot of teams, fixtures and competitions at once, this is the tier for you.

## How to decide

If you're trying MatchPulse for the first time, stay on Free until you hit a limit. If you run competitions often, Plus pays for itself quickly as a once-off. If you're running a league or association at scale, Pro is the one.

When you're ready, see [Upgrade to Plus or Pro](../09-billing/upgrade.md). Payments are handled through PayFast.
