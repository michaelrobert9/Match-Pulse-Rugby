# Link a parent organisation

A parent organisation connects a smaller organisation to a bigger one. A school links to its association. A club links to the body that runs its league. This is what lets a wider organisation run competitions that include teams from many members.

## When you need this

Set a parent if your organisation plays in competitions run by a higher body. If your school competes in an association league, link the association as the parent. If your organisation is fully independent, you don't need a parent at all.

## How to link

1. Open the organisation you want to link.
2. Go to its **settings**.
3. Find the **parent organisation** field.
4. Select the organisation that should be the parent.
5. Save.

The link is now in place. Your organisation keeps managing its own teams; the parent can now include those teams in its competitions.

## Things to know

- A parent link is optional. Plenty of organisations run perfectly well without one.
- The parent doesn't take over your organisation. You still own and manage your own teams and any competitions you run yourself.
- If your structure changes, you can update or remove the parent link from the same settings.
