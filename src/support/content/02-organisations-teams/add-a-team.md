# Add a team

A team belongs to an organisation and is what you enter into competitions. You'll usually create several within one organisation, one for each side you run.

## Before you start

Have your naming sorted. A team's name appears in fixtures and standings, so it's worth being consistent. See [How teams are created and named](how-teams-are-named.md) for guidance.

## Steps

1. Open the organisation the team belongs to.
2. Go to the **Teams** section.
3. Click **Add team**.
4. Enter the **team name** (for example, "Riverside College 1st XI").
5. Add any other details you're prompted for, such as an age group or logo.
6. Click **Save**.

The team now appears under your organisation and is ready to be entered into a competition.

## Adding several teams

If you run multiple sides, repeat the steps for each. Keep the naming format consistent across all of them so they read cleanly when they appear side by side.
