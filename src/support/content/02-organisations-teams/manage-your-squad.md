# Manage your squad and players

A team's squad is its list of players. Players are what link tries, kicks and cards to individuals, which is how player and career stats build up over time.

## Add a player

1. Open the team.
2. Go to the **Squad** or **Players** section.
3. Click **Add player**.
4. Enter the player's name and any other details you're prompted for.
5. Save.

Repeat for each player in the squad.

## Edit a player

Open the player from the squad list, click **Edit**, change what you need, and save. This is how you fix a misspelt name or update a player's details.

## Remove a player

Remove a player from the squad list when they're no longer part of the team. Their past stats stay attached to the matches they actually played; removing them from the current squad just means they won't appear in upcoming line-ups.

## Why the squad matters for stats

When you score a match live, scores and events get attributed to players. That's only possible if the players are in the squad. A complete, correctly spelt squad means clean player stats and accurate career records. See [Player and career stats](../07-stats-standings/player-and-career-stats.md) for how those build up.

## Tip

Set up the squad before the season starts rather than mid-match. Scrambling to add a player while a game is live is avoidable hassle.
