# Archive or remove a team

When a team is no longer active, you can take it out of the way without losing its history.

## Archive vs remove

- **Archive** keeps the team and all its history, but tucks it out of your active lists. This is the safe option for a side that's done for the season but might come back, or one whose past results you want to keep on record.
- **Remove** takes the team out entirely.

Archive is almost always the better choice. A team carries results, stats and a place in past competitions. Keeping it archived means none of that history is disturbed.

## How to archive

1. Open the organisation.
2. Go to the **Teams** section.
3. Open the team.
4. Choose **Archive**.

The team moves out of your active teams but stays on record.

## How to remove

1. Open the team as above.
2. Choose **Remove** or **Delete**.
3. Confirm.

## Before you remove a team

Think twice if the team has played fixtures. Its results feed standings and stats in any competition it took part in. Removing an active team can leave gaps. If you just want it out of sight, archive it instead.

If a team is still entered in a live competition, take it out of that competition properly rather than deleting it outright. For non-standard exits like a mid-season withdrawal, see [Walkovers, withdrawals and abandonments](../04-fixtures/non-standard-outcomes.md).
