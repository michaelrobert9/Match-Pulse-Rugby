# Create an organisation

An organisation holds your teams and is where you run competitions from. You can create more than one if you manage several.

## Steps

1. From your dashboard, click **Create organisation**.
2. Choose the **type**: School, Club, Association or Franchise. (See [Org types](org-types.md) if you're unsure.)
3. Enter the **name** of the organisation.
4. Add any extra details you're prompted for, such as a logo or location.
5. Click **Save**.

You're now the Owner of this organisation, and it shows on your dashboard.

## After you create it

- If it belongs to a wider body, [link a parent organisation](link-a-parent-organisation.md).
- Start [adding teams](add-a-team.md).
- When you're ready to run matches, [create a competition](../03-competitions/create-a-competition.md).

## Running more than one organisation

There's nothing stopping you from creating several organisations. If you manage a school and also help run the local association, each is its own organisation with its own teams and competitions. Your dashboard shows all the ones you own or have a role in.
