# Edit a team

You can change a team's details at any time, including its name, logo and other information.

## Steps

1. Open the organisation the team belongs to.
2. Go to the **Teams** section.
3. Find the team and open it.
4. Click **Edit**.
5. Update the details you want to change.
6. Click **Save**.

## A note on renaming

If you rename a team, the new name carries through everywhere it appears, including fixtures and standings. The team itself stays the same; only its name changes, so its results and stats stay attached to it.

Be a little careful renaming a team mid-competition. The change is fine and won't break anything, but it can confuse people who knew the team by its old name. If you do it, a quick heads-up to anyone scoring that competition helps.

## What you can't change here

A team's results and stats aren't edited from this screen. Those come from finalised fixtures and recompute from the source. If a stat looks wrong, the fix is at the fixture level, not the team level. See [Why a stat looks wrong](../07-stats-standings/why-a-stat-looks-wrong.md).
