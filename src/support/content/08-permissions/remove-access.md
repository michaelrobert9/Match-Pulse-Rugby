# Remove someone's access

When a coach moves on, a season ends, or someone no longer needs to help, you can take away their access. Only Owners can do this.

## Steps

1. Open the competition or team they have access to.
2. Go to the **People** or **Roles** section.
3. Find the person.
4. Choose **Remove** (or change their role).
5. Confirm.

Their access ends, and the competition or team no longer appears on their dashboard.

## Change instead of remove

You don't always need to remove someone entirely. If their job is changing rather than ending, adjust their role or scope instead. For example, narrow a competition-wide Scorer down to a single team, or move someone from Owner-level to Scorer. See [Roles explained](roles-explained.md) and [Scoped roles](scoped-roles.md).

## What removing doesn't do

Removing someone's access doesn't undo the work they did. Matches they scored stay scored, and results they finalised stay in place. You're taking away what they can do from now on, not erasing what's already happened.

## Good practice

- **Tidy up at season's end.** Remove access people no longer need, so your list of who-can-do-what stays accurate.
- **Keep Owner-level access tight.** The fewer people with full authority, the clearer your control of the competition stays.
