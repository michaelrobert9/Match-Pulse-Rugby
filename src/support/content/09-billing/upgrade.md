# Upgrade to Plus or Pro

When you're ready for more than the Free plan offers, you can upgrade to Plus or Pro. Payments go through PayFast.

## The options

- **Plus, R2,000 once-off.** A single payment, not a subscription. Pay once and Plus stays unlocked.
- **Pro, R15,000 per year.** Billed annually, for running multiple competitions at scale.

For who each suits, see [Choosing a plan](../01-getting-started/choosing-a-plan.md).

## How to upgrade

1. Go to the [Plans page](https://matchpulse.co.za/plans).
2. Check what each tier includes and pick the one you want.
3. Choose **Upgrade** on that plan.
4. You'll be taken to PayFast to complete payment.
5. Pay using your preferred PayFast method.
6. Once payment confirms, your plan unlocks.

## After paying

Your account updates to the new plan once PayFast confirms the payment. If it doesn't seem to have unlocked, give it a moment, payment confirmation can take a little time to come back. If it still hasn't, see [Manage your subscription](manage-subscription.md) or get in touch via the [troubleshooting](../10-troubleshooting/something-isnt-working.md) page.
