# Manage your subscription

You can check your current plan and sort out anything to do with billing from your account.

## Check your current plan

1. Open your account or billing settings.
2. Your active plan, Free, Plus or Pro, is shown there.

## Plus: the once-off plan

Plus is a single payment, so there's no recurring subscription to manage. Once it's paid and unlocked, it stays active. Nothing renews and nothing needs cancelling.

## Pro: the annual plan

Pro renews each year. Check your billing settings for your renewal details. If you want to change or stop the annual renewal, do it from there before the renewal date.

## Payment didn't unlock my plan

If you've paid but the plan hasn't activated:

1. Give it a few minutes, confirmation from PayFast can take a moment.
2. Refresh the page.
3. Check your billing settings to see whether the payment registered.

If it still hasn't come through after that, reach out via [Something isn't working](../10-troubleshooting/something-isnt-working.md) with the details of your payment, and we'll sort it out.

## Changing plans

To move from Free to Plus or Pro, see [Upgrade to Plus or Pro](upgrade.md). To understand which plan fits you, see [Choosing a plan](../01-getting-started/choosing-a-plan.md).
