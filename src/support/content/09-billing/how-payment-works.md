# How payment works

MatchPulse handles payments through PayFast, a South African payment gateway. You don't pay MatchPulse directly with card details; PayFast processes the payment and confirms it back.

## The flow

1. You choose a plan to upgrade to on the [Plans page](/plans).
2. MatchPulse sends you to PayFast to pay.
3. You complete payment with your chosen method on PayFast.
4. PayFast confirms the payment back to MatchPulse.
5. Your plan unlocks on your account.

That confirmation step is automatic. Once PayFast tells MatchPulse the payment went through, your account updates.

## What you're paying for

- **Plus** is a once-off payment. You pay one time and the plan stays active. There's nothing recurring to manage.
- **Pro** is an annual payment, renewed each year.

## If confirmation is slow

Payment confirmation usually comes through quickly, but it can take a short while. If your plan hasn't unlocked right after paying, wait a few minutes and refresh. If it still hasn't updated, check [Manage your subscription](manage-subscription.md) or reach out via [Something isn't working](../10-troubleshooting/something-isnt-working.md).

## Security

Your payment details are handled by PayFast, not stored by MatchPulse. PayFast is an established South African gateway built for exactly this.
