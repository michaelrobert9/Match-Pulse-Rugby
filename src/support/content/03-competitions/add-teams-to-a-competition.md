# Add teams to a competition

A competition needs teams before you can schedule any fixtures. Here's how to enter them.

## Before you start

The teams should already exist in an organisation. If they don't, [add them first](../02-organisations-teams/add-a-team.md).

## Steps

1. Open the competition.
2. Go to the **Teams** section.
3. Click **Add team**.
4. Select the team you want to enter.
5. Repeat for each team.
6. Save.

The teams are now part of the competition and will appear in fixtures and standings.

## Adding teams from other organisations

If your competition is run by an association or a wider body, you can enter teams that belong to member organisations. This is where the parent organisation link matters: it's what connects member schools and clubs to the body running the competition. See [Link a parent organisation](../02-organisations-teams/link-a-parent-organisation.md).

## After teams are in

- [Add fixtures](../04-fixtures/add-a-fixture.md) one by one, or
- [Generate a full fixture list](../04-fixtures/generate-fixtures.md) automatically from the teams you've entered.

## Removing a team from a competition

If a team needs to come out, remove it from the competition's Teams section. Be careful doing this once fixtures have been played, since it affects standings. For a team that withdraws partway through, handle it as a withdrawal so the results stay correct. See [Walkovers, withdrawals and abandonments](../04-fixtures/non-standard-outcomes.md).
