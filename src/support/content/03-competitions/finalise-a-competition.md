# Finalise a competition

When every fixture has been played and scored, you finalise the competition. This closes it out and locks in the final standings and stats.

## Before you finalise

Make sure:

- All fixtures have a result, or have been resolved as a non-standard outcome (walkover, withdrawal, abandonment). See [Walkovers, withdrawals and abandonments](../04-fixtures/non-standard-outcomes.md).
- The standings look right. They recompute from the results, so if every fixture is finalised, the table should be settled.
- Any corrections are done. It's cleaner to fix a wrong result before finalising than after.

## Steps

1. Open the competition.
2. Confirm all fixtures are complete.
3. Choose **Finalise competition**.
4. Confirm.

## What finalising does

Finalising marks the competition as complete and settles its records. The competition stats are written, and the final standings stand as the season's result. Player career stats also reflect what happened in this competition.

## If you need to change something after finalising

You can still correct a mistake, but it's more involved than fixing it beforehand. Because stats recompute from the source results, correcting a finalised fixture and re-running the calculations will update the records properly. See [Correct a finalised result](../06-live-scoring/correct-a-finalised-result.md) and [Why a stat looks wrong](../07-stats-standings/why-a-stat-looks-wrong.md).

The cleaner path is always to get everything right before you finalise.
