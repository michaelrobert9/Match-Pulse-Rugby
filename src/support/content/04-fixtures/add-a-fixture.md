# Add a fixture

A fixture is a single match between two teams. You can add them one at a time, which is handy for a friendly or a one-off, or generate a whole list at once for a league.

## Before you start

The two teams need to be in the competition already. If they're not, [add them first](../03-competitions/add-teams-to-a-competition.md).

## Steps

1. Open the competition.
2. Go to the **Fixtures** section.
3. Click **Add fixture**.
4. Choose the **home team** and the **away team**.
5. Set the **date and time**.
6. Add a **venue** if you have one.
7. Save.

The fixture now appears in the fixture list, ready to be played and scored.

## Adding several fixtures

If you're setting up a full round-robin where everyone plays everyone, generating the fixtures is faster than adding them one by one. See [Generate a full fixture list](generate-fixtures.md).

## What happens next

A new fixture starts at the beginning of its lifecycle, waiting to be played. When the day comes, you [start it live](../06-live-scoring/start-a-live-match.md) and score it. To understand the stages a fixture moves through, see [The fixture lifecycle](fixture-lifecycle.md).

## Changing a fixture later

Dates move and venues change. You can edit any of this after the fact. See [Edit a fixture](edit-a-fixture.md), or [Postpone or reschedule](postpone-or-reschedule.md).
