# Edit a fixture

You can change a fixture's details, including its date, time, venue and teams. What you can change depends on where the fixture is in its lifecycle.

## Steps

1. Open the competition.
2. Go to the **Fixtures** section.
3. Find the fixture and open it.
4. Click **Edit**.
5. Change the details you need.
6. Save.

## What you can change

- **Date and time.** Move a fixture to a new slot. For a proper postponement, see [Postpone or reschedule](postpone-or-reschedule.md).
- **Venue.** Update where it's played.
- **Teams.** Change the home or away team if a fixture was set up wrong. Take care doing this once the fixture has been played.

## Editing depends on the fixture's status

A fixture that hasn't been played is freely editable. One that's live or finished behaves differently, because changing it affects the result and the standings. If you need to change the score of a match that's already finished, that's a result correction, not a simple edit. See [Correct a finalised result](../06-live-scoring/correct-a-finalised-result.md).

To understand which stage a fixture is in and what that allows, see [The fixture lifecycle](fixture-lifecycle.md).

## For non-standard outcomes

If a match didn't play out normally, a walkover, a withdrawal, or an abandonment, don't force it through the edit screen. There's a proper way to record each of these. See [Walkovers, withdrawals and abandonments](non-standard-outcomes.md).
