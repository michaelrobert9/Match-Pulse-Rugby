# Fix a mistake during a match

Scoring live means the odd slip: a goal to the wrong team, the wrong scorer, or one logged that shouldn't have been. While the match is live, these are quick to put right.

## Fix a goal

1. Find the goal in the match's event list.
2. Edit it to correct the team or scorer, or remove it if it shouldn't be there.
3. The score adjusts to match.

## Common fixes

- **Wrong scorer, right team:** edit the goal and change the player. The team score stays the same; the player credit moves.
- **Wrong team:** edit the goal to the correct team, or remove and re-add it.
- **Goal that shouldn't count:** remove it. The score drops back.

## Do it while you're live

The best time to fix a scoring mistake is during the match, before you finalise. Everything's still open and a correction is just an edit.

Once the match is finalised, the result is locked and feeds the standings and stats. Fixing it then is a result correction, which is more involved. See [Correct a finalised result](correct-a-finalised-result.md).

## Before you finalise

When the match ends, take a moment to check the score and the scorers look right before locking it in. A quick review at full time saves a correction later. See [Finalise a result](finalise-a-result.md).
