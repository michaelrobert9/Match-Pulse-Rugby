# How teams advance

In a knockout, the winner of each match moves on to the next round. MatchPulse handles this for you as results are finalised, so you don't have to move teams forward by hand.

## How it works

1. You play and score a knockout fixture, the same as any other match.
2. When you finalise the result, MatchPulse knows who won.
3. The winner is placed into the next round's fixture automatically.
4. Once both feeder matches in a round are finalised, the next-round matchup is complete and ready to be played.

So a quarter-final winner lands in the right semi-final slot, and the two semi-final winners meet in the final, all driven by the results you enter.

## What you still do

- **Score the matches** and finalise them. That's what triggers the advance.
- **Set dates and venues** for the next round once the matchups are known.

## If a match can't be a draw

Knockout matches need a winner. If your competition allows draws in normal play, decide in advance how knockout ties are settled, by your competition's rules, and record the winning side so the right team advances.

## If the wrong team advanced

That almost always means a result was entered wrong. Don't try to drag the team back by editing the bracket. Correct the original result instead, and the advance updates to match. See [Correct a finalised result](../06-live-scoring/correct-a-finalised-result.md).
