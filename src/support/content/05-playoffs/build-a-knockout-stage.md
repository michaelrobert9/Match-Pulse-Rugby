# Build a knockout stage

A knockout stage is a bracket of elimination matches. Win and you advance, lose and you're out, until one team is left. You build it with the playoff builder, either as a standalone knockout competition or as the finals stage of a hybrid competition.

## When you build one

- **Standalone knockout:** the whole competition is a bracket. Build it once your teams are in.
- **Hybrid finals:** a league or group phase runs first, then the top teams go into a knockout. Build this stage once the qualifying teams are known.

See [Competition formats](../03-competitions/competition-formats.md) for the difference.

## Before you start

- The teams that will play in the knockout should be ready.
- For a hybrid, the league phase should be far enough along that you know who's qualified.

## Steps

1. Open the competition.
2. Go to the **Playoffs** or **Knockout** section.
3. Choose **Build knockout stage**.
4. Set the number of teams and the bracket shape (for example, quarter-finals into semis into a final).
5. Add the teams into the bracket. You can seed them so the bracket is balanced. See [Seed the bracket](seed-the-bracket.md).
6. Save.

MatchPulse lays out the rounds and creates the fixtures for the first round.

## After building

- Check the matchups look right.
- Set dates, times and venues for the first-round fixtures, the same as any other fixture.
- As results come in, winners advance to the next round automatically. See [How teams advance](how-teams-advance.md).

## Changing it later

If you set the bracket up wrong before any matches are played, you can edit it. See [Edit the bracket](edit-the-bracket.md).
