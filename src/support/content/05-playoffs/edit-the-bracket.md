# Edit the bracket

You can change a knockout bracket, but how much you can change depends on whether matches have already been played.

## Before any matches are played

The bracket is fully editable. You can:

- Change the seeding and move teams between positions.
- Swap a team in or out.
- Adjust the bracket shape if you set it up wrong.

1. Open the competition's **Playoffs** section.
2. Open the bracket.
3. Make your changes.
4. Save.

This is the best time to fix anything. Get the bracket right before the first round kicks off.

## Once matches have started

After results come in, the bracket is partly settled. Teams that have won have advanced, and changing the structure underneath them gets messy. Avoid reshaping a bracket mid-tournament unless you have to.

If a first-round result was scored wrong and the wrong team advanced, the fix is to correct that result, not to rearrange the bracket by hand. Correcting the result updates who advances. See [Correct a finalised result](../06-live-scoring/correct-a-finalised-result.md).

## A note on advancing

You don't manually move winners forward in a normal knockout. As each match is finalised, the winner advances to the next round on its own. See [How teams advance](how-teams-advance.md). The bracket editor is for setting up the structure, not for forcing results.
